Recent Work

01 / SentiGauge

Data Driven Web App

SentiGauge is a web-based sentiment analysis platform that monitors various social media feeds for the mention of user-defined keywords. SentiGauge extracts social media data from Facebook, Twitter and RSS news feeds and calculates the sentiment of the data (i.e. whether the article or post is positive, negative or neutral). This data is then aggregated and reported on.
